Explore the Wonders of Taniti

From sun-kissed beaches and lush rainforests to exciting adventures and cultural experiences, Taniti offers something for every traveler. Discover the heart of our island!

Pristine Beaches

Relax on Taniti's famous white sandy beaches encircling Yellow Leaf Bay, or explore more secluded rocky coves. Perfect for swimming, sunbathing, snorkeling, and surfing.

Rainforest Exploration

Hike through lush tropical rainforests, discover hidden waterfalls, and encounter exotic flora and fauna.

Visit the Volcano

Embark on an adventurous trek to Taniti's small, active volcano. The summit offers breathtaking panoramic views of the island and the surrounding Pacific Ocean.

Cultural & Historical Sites

Discover Taniti City's native architecture, visit the local history museum, and explore art galleries. Experience the island's rich heritage through bus or boat tours.

Water Adventures

Dive into adventure with chartered fishing tours, snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, and scuba diving to explore vibrant coral reefs and marine life.

Merriton Landing Fun

This rapidly developing area offers several pubs (including a microbrewery), a new dance club, a cinema, helicopter rides, an arcade, and bowling. A nine-hole golf course is coming soon!
