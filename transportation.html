Getting Around

Taniti offers a variety of transportation options to help you explore our beautiful island with ease. Whether you prefer public transit, the freedom of a rental, or a leisurely bike ride, we've got you covered.

Arriving in Taniti

Image: Airplane

Arrival by Air

Almost all visitors arrive by air. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. The airport is currently being expanded to welcome larger jets in the coming years.

Image: Cruise Ship

Arrival by Sea

A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week, offering another way for some visitors to experience Taniti.

Travel Options

Public & Private Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily. Private buses provide service to the rest of the island, offering an affordable way to reach various destinations.

Taxis

Taxis are readily available in Taniti City, providing a convenient option for direct travel to your destination. Ideal for quick trips or when you have luggage.

Rental Cars

For maximum flexibility and freedom to explore Taniti at your own pace, rental cars are available from a local agency near the airport.

Bike Rentals

Enjoy the scenic beauty of Taniti with a bike rental. Bikes and helmets are available from several vendors. Taniti City's flat terrain makes it perfect for cycling. Note: Helmets are required by law for your safety.
